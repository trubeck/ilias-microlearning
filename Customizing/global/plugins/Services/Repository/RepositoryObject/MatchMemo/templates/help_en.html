The rules for playing "Match & Memo"

  • The "cards" are layed in rows, face down.
  • Turn over any two cards by clicking on the back of the card. The card content will be shown in a separate box which can be moved with the mouse.
  • If the two cards match, they will vanish from the playing field.
  • If the two cards don't match, they will be turned over back to their original positions when you click anywhere with the mouse.
  • Remember what was on each card and where it was.
  • The game is over when all the cards have been matched and vanished from the playing field.
  • After the game you may enter your name or your initials into a highscore ranking.
